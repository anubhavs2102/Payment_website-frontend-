Corporate Social Responsibility

One97 Communications believes in combining the power of technology to build an inclusive culture and lay a greater emphasis on the growth as well as social welfare of the communities in which we live and work.

The aim is to create opportunities for the marginalized communities by facilitating quality education, creating a financially inclusive society, promoting a healthy society, reduction of carbon footprint and disaster management. As a financial institution we are strengthened to bring positive change in the lives of the socially disadvantaged communities across the country.

With respect to Companies Act, 2013, One 97 communications invests in various CSR initiatives through Paytm Foundation. By responding to the emerging needs at grassroots level, we are focused on specifically identifying and executing activities which are in tandem with the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals
